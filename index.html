About Us

Providing an efficient and effective solution to be integrated with “Govi Gedara” digital platform for better buying and selling experience which arises opportunity for small-scale farmers and home gardeners to sell their products at a reasonable price and for customers to fulfill their food requirements with fresh fruits and vegetables from the nearest dealer.

Our Services

Main Purpose of This APP

  • Giving same opportunity to small-scale farmers and home gardeners
  • Reducing the time waste for shopping.
  • Encouraging people to stay home safely (Because of Covid-19 crisis in Sri Lanka).
  • Building up a platform which connects directly sellers and buyers instead of middlemen.

Who can Access This

Three main characters

  • Seller

    • Seller can create a store by displaying several items that he has to sell. He can accept orders and list bids also.

  • Buyer

    • Buyers can search products available from different stores by typing keywords on search bar. If he/she satisfied, they can add relevant products to the cart. They can ask for bids too.

  • Deliver

    • They are three main suppling methods. They are Buyer delivery, Seller delivery and 3rd party delivery. Buyers can collect their order by themselves. It is buyer delivery method and it has no change.
